Frequently Asked Questions

### Question 1
**Q:** How long does the Strengths Programme take?
**A:** Most programs are completed over 3-6 months, with ongoing support available as needed. The exact timeline depends on the specific program and your organization's needs.

### Question 2
**Q:** What assessment tool do you use?
**A:** We use proven strengths assessment tools including CliftonStrengths (formerly StrengthsFinder) and other validated instruments to identify your unique combination of strengths.

### Question 3
**Q:** Can programs be customized for our organization?
**A:** Yes, all programs can be customized to fit your organization's unique needs, industry, and objectives. We work with you to create a tailored solution.

### Question 4
**Q:** What results can we expect?
**A:** Organizations typically see improvements in employee engagement (40%+), team collaboration (85% report improvements), productivity (25%+), and reduced turnover (30% reduction).

### Question 5
**Q:** Do you offer virtual or in-person programs?
**A:** We offer both virtual and in-person programs to accommodate your team's preferences and location. Hybrid options are also available.
